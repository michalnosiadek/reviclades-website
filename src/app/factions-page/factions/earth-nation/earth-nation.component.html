Earth Nation

Description

Nations build on hard work and incredible minds. People here value creativity the most, and not only when it comes to art. Artists, Builders, Architects, Scientist - all of these professions are highly valued in this society. Each voter chooses the leader based on their inventions. It doesn’t matter how high is someone’s social status, what matters is the mind. Most of earth citizens live in cities inside the largest canyon on the continent, and because of that, those places are crowded with locals and citizens. With all these incredible minds and the biggest magical resources on the continent, Earth nation gave birth to many talented mages. Magic is important to the point that even technology is fuelled up by magic, every sculpture or other invention too. Majority of magic items are produced there, just like most of the metals and art pieces. With such a powerful domestic resources, these lands have been main trade hub for many years.

Your mind is sharp. When it comes to magic you call yourself a natural. Always trying to find innovative ways to save problems. You never help if there is no reward. Discriminate mind, not the body.
