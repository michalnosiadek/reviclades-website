Gorantuland

Description

Proud nation of combatant scholars that lives in the forest as its guardians. In the past two divided races fought with each-other over the absolute control of the area. In the meantime, Dark Lord dispensed monstrosities across whole forest. Quickly war changed to battle for survival. Two races overwhelmed by foreign enemy, joined forces. Even though they worked together, nothing could stop the massacre of their people, so united races decided to gamble. By sacraficing 75% of their population in blood ritual, those two races became one. With the new mutated power they destroy all hostile creatures. That was the born of Gorantulan race. Now they stand as a strong keepers of the forest, with high intellect and superior physique Gorantulans became on of the seven forces in the world. School system so extraordinary that every youngling want to attend there. Gorantulans might look intimidating, but they respect every race as equal. Their core rule is to try and enhance potential in every creature. Gorantulans even started to teach leanguage to the non-intelligent beast known as Ferals and co-exist with them on daily basis.
