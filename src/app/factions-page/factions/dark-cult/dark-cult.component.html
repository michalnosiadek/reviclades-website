Dark Cult

Description

Dangerous and clever people that often use cult as a means for their own goals. Consist of murderers, thieves, politicians and mostly of people that would do anything just to benefit themselves. When people dissapear, often it is because of Dark Cult influence. This organization use sacrifices to communicate and worship Dark Lord. Many say that you can’t find the cult, it finds you. There are military structures inside of the cult. Normal worshippers are able to do what they want without any responsibilities, but the higher-ups often are bound by many rules. Infamous 7 Deadly Sins represent the worst of whole cult, even called demi-gods by worshippers. Their identity is secret and only closest allies are let near them. Because of the all mysteries around them, some nations doesn’t officially believe in them. Cultists can be found in every society in the world, but identifying them can be difficult. It can be your neighbour, you brother or even your pet in disguise. Sometimes it is better to not know.

Your goal, only that matters. If you need to kill someone, rob or abduct it doesn’t matter. You are truly free without any restrictions.
