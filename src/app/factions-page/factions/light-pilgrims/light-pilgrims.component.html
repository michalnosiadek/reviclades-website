Light Pilgrims / Light Enclave

Description

There is not a lot information about Light Pilgrims whereabouts. Merchants and travelers that met pilgrims in their city never remembers how to get there. Some say somewhere in the desert you can find them. Most famous for their glass making and drug production. Led by blind prophet, called Father of Light, they do not interfere in world matters. They strongly believe in the Sun Goddess that protects them from harm. High Pilgrims that met their Goddess are always blind, which is considered as biggest privilege. Pilgrims do not care about physical commodities like belongings or money. They love to travel and spread world of their Goddess. Wary of revealing any information about their culture.
