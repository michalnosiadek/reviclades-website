Fire Folk

Description

As an old fire warrior from the ancient time of elementals, Hector does not follow the new age religion of fire. Even though his memory is foggy, remembering teaching of his god Hector wanders the fire wastelands alone, challenging any worthy opponent. With his pride of a true soldier, he never attacks creatures unwilling of fight. At times he helps travelers and merchants in trouble. Gloomy without a fight, showing that time took a toll on him, yet still glowing like a bright wildfire when battle begins.

You are person with a strong belief to your God. While not aggressive, you will not ignore people in need. Fire has special place in your heart, somehow its more pure than most of people.
