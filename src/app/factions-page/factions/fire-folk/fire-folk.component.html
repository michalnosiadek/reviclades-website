Fire Folk

Description

Conservative theocracy that value piousness like nothing else. With their strong belief in the Fire God Fornax they give most of valuables to holy church led by the Immortal Bishop. Known for their absence when it comes to violence, they cherish fire lands rich in fertile grounds. Farms can be seen on various places throughout the nation. People of this nation rarely leave their homes to see the world. Fire grounds connect with believers and protect them in the time of need. Although fire people are nice and helpful, they can get quite hostile while arguing about their beliefs.

You are person with a strong belief to your God. While not aggressive, you will not ignore people in need. Fire has special place in your heart, somehow its more pure than most of people.
