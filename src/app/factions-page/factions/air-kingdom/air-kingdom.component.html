Air Kingdom

Description

Ruled by two mighty kings, Air Kingdom is largest nation in the world. Backed by aristocrats, Kings always represent two branches: Military and Science. Now majority of aristocrats families back military king, because of that military power is growing day by day. Placed on the desert, neighbouring to The Great Wastes, kingdom must tackle food and water supply issues. Import is crucial and without it it cannot supply any crucial resources. Specialized in technology development people of air found a way to live on the deserted land, cities build on clouds. Those cities were built by lowest cast, slaves. Slavery is common and somehow respected in the kingdom. There are four casts in total: Aristocrats, Commoners, Nomads, Slaves. Aristocrats lives in the biggest cities and are the lords of society, that treat slaves as disposable tools. Commoners can be found in every city in the kingdom. They tend to treat everyone fairly, often commoners are bridge between aristocrats and other casts. Nomads live underneath settlements on clouds, because they rarely are let inside cities. Known for their talent for receiving technology from the Old World, that aristocrats are found of, Nomads live hard life in the shadow of their rulers. Throughout Nomads societies there is deep believe in Air God, that aristocrats despite and try to ban religious believes. Last cast, the slaves, live mainly in big cities serving the true citizens. There are more slaves than other casts combined. Powerless against technology of rulers, they are serving obediently for ages without any major revolts. Slaves also pray to Air God with the hope to be freed from the suffering. Even though there are many different societies in kingdom, the army control all areas. With high taxes and biggest resources of artefacts Air Kingdom is called the riches nation.

You are person that is loyal to your kingdom and believe that authorities can help you in need. Using other people for your means is common. Technology for you was always more convenient than magic. Money is power and with power you can accomplish anything.
