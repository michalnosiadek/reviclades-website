Fire Berserker

Forgotten Ruthless Warrior

As an old fire warrior from the ancient time of elementals, Hector does not follow the new age religion of fire. Even though his memory is foggy, remembering teaching of his god, Hector wanders the fire wastelands alone, challenging any worthy opponent.

With his pride of a true soldier, he never attacks creatures unwilling of fight. At times he helps travelers and merchants in trouble. Gloomy without a fight, showing that time took a toll on him, yet still glowing like a bright wildfire when battle begins.

As the Fire Elemental’s strength wanes and its soul flickers out, it erupts in a final, wrathful blaze. The lingering embers of its essence ignite in a searing explosion, engulfing everything within the blast radius in purging flames.

With a mighty, ground-shattering swing, the Fire Berserker unleashes a surge of molten fury, striking all enemies in his vicinity. Those caught in the attack feel the raw power of an ancient warrior whose wrath burns hotter than the core of the earth itself.

Gorantulan Hunter

Pure-hearted Hunter of the Gorantulans

As a young adult from the Bazula family of mighty hunters, Banzai was trained from an early stage of his live to guard Gorantulan’s home.

„I, Huntsman Banzai, promise to always defend Gorantuland land and its people from dangers. With might of all predecessors I will cherish this land. Since I am a hunter, my hunt will be over only after my death.” ~ Part of Banazai’s Hunter Oath

Ability 1: Jungle Snare

Sets a hidden trap that immobilizes unwary foes.

Ability 2: Beastly Bond

Summons a Gorantulan companion to assist in battle.

Ability 3: Heart of the Wild

Temporarily boosts agility and senses, allowing precise strikes.

Shrimp Monk

Greedy martial artist

At first glance you can’t tell what kind of person Master Tempura is. Looking like a humble monk, he attracts many people willing to learn Tempura’s craft.

Maybe it’s expensive but at least hard and demanding! But what you expect from the shrimp that survived on deserts for years and trained physical might with Gorantulans. Now it’s your chance to become like him!” ~ Advertisement heard from a shrimp-like person in the village near the Grand Canyon

Ability 1: Shell Kick

Unleashes a powerful kick reinforced by hardened shell chi.

Ability 2: Greedy Grab

Attempts to snatch valuables or siphon enemy energy mid-combat.

Ability 3: Tidal Palm

Channels water chi for a massive palm strike, pushing foes away.

Bat Priest

Cunning priestess of the Dark Lord

Bahrana is one of those rare cases when a Feral becomes intelligent to the point she can be called a genius.

In the past, one individual saved her life out of pure accident and decided to teach her their ways of living. Magic, languages, etiquette, fighting, alchemy and many other things that helped Bahrana in the future. With these skills she started to worship vigorously the Dark Lord. By deceiving people into joining the cult, promising immortality and chance to see again her dead relatives, she devours her victims. Each meal makes her stronger. Her appetite is infinite.

Ability 1: Echolocation Burst

Releases a sonic wave, stunning enemies in its path.

Ability 2: Dark Prayer

Channels forbidden magic to heal allies or harm foes.

Ability 3: Winged Ascent

Temporarily takes flight, gaining high mobility and vantage.

Crypt Shambler

Mechanical construct overtaken by madness

“Here as you can see is one of the typical mining models from the old world’s Transmigation project. A drill to destroy stone matter under cities shattered by earthquake. Strong tail to hold stones and the ceiling of tunnels. Claws to cut metal cables and lines.

Just imagine if this specimen was alive! This is really a miracle of the past people! Look at it and remember that even advanced technology can’t win over time.” ~ Tour Guide in Museum of Lost Memories inside Ape Nation

Ability 1: Rusted Slash

A decaying blade that inflicts lingering damage over time.

Ability 2: Unholy Gears

Temporarily overclocks its mechanical limbs for rapid strikes.

Ability 3: Necro Pulse

Emits a wave of dark energy that reanimates nearby corpses.
