Cartographer

A mysterious individual that illustrates the world’s wonders and creates detailed maps of the continent. Everyone heard the title “Wondrous Brush”, but no one actually saw this persona. Many speculate that this cannot be one person, because traveling and creating such wonders takes a lot of time. One is clear. There is no better cartographer in the world.
