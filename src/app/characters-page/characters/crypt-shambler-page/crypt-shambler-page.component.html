Crypt Shambler

Lore

In the era of humanity there was a man with unhinged nature. Killing was like another sports discipline to him. In any possible way, using equipment so strange that detectives were confused when seeing victim’s body.

He got unlucky once and got captured. Because of his crimes he received a trial suited for such a monster.

At the time technology was so advanced that human’s mind could be transported to physical discs and then even robots. His punishment was hard labor work, but not in his human form. Instead his mind was transported everyday to mining robot unit.

During calamity, his body was destroyed and mind trapped in a robot forever. Before energy ran out, he crumbled to insanity. With nothing to kill, not even time, he went to deep slumber.

After many years, a strange sensation woke him up. What he saw was many eerie creatures and people. He killed all of them and run as far as possible.

“Here as you can see is one of the typical mining models from the old world’s Transmigation project. A drill to destroy stone matter under cities shattered by earthquake. Strong tail to hold stones and the ceiling of tunnels. Claws to cut metal cables and lines.

Just imagine if this specimen was alive! This is really a miracle of the past people! Look at it and remember that even advanced technology can’t win over time.” ~ Tour Guide in Museum of Lost Memories inside Ape Nation
