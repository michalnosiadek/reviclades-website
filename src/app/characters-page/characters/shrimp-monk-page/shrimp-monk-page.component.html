Shrimp Monk

Lore

At first glance you can’t tell what kind of person Master Tempura is. Looking like a humble monk, he attracts many people willing to learn Tempura’s craft.

True goal is different from just spreading knowledge. Money. Money is what matters for Master Tempura. Because his sensei was a humble martial art legend, Tempura wants to live a different life to escape his sensei’s shadow. Even after helping someone in distress, he expects a reward.

He never stays in one place, scams people whenever he can, avoids fighting and resolves issues with money. That’s Master Tempura for you!

„Come here good people! Today starts the recruitment for the Master Tempura travelling martial arts school.

Maybe it’s expensive but at least hard and demanding! But what you expect from the shrimp that survived on deserts for years and trained physical might with Gorantulans. Now it’s your chance to become like him!” ~ Advertisement heard from a shrimp-like person in the village near the Grand Canyon
