Fire Berserker

Lore

As an old fire warrior from the ancient time of elementals, Hector does not follow the new age religion of fire. Even though his memory is foggy, remembering teaching of his god, Hector wanders the fire wastelands alone, challenging any worthy opponent.

With his pride of a true soldier, he never attacks creatures unwilling of fight. At times he helps travelers and merchants in trouble. Gloomy without a fight, showing that time took a toll on him, yet still glowing like a bright wildfire when battle begins.

„Hector is an ancient fire elemental that still remembers true nature of the fire people. He wanders volcanic landscapes to find worthy opponents. His will to fight burns deeply inside him, just like volcano. Mostly calm, but deadly when the heat goes up.” ~ One of the farmers working on Fire Folk grounds.
