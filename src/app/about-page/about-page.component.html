About us

The Reviclades team consists of dedicated members experienced in programming, art and creative design. The idea for the game was born simply as a result of a player driven experience and a desire to undertake a challenge of creating something with pure passion. We are highly focused on every single player’s opinions to showcase a game that YOU want to play.

Our Story

We have started as a small gaming idea which would allow players to participate in the creation a game with developers. Nowadays, everyone is aware that games are being created focusing on the profit while ignoring the gameplay, artstyle and players. We want to make a change. Each and every aspect you see inside the Reviclades was closely discussed with the community and internally with our team. We hope to deliver the game that YOU want to play.

Every substantial project is initially born as a small idea. The Reviclades is a video game project where players help develop that small idea into something great. We put our outmost care to the community that is a crucial part of our strategy. Our goal is to make competetive arena games relevant and to spread fantastic wonders of the Reviclades’ world.

The Reviclades is a competitive arena PVP strategic game set in the world destroyed and set anew by calamities. Here, the arena is a saint ground where people resolve matters between them. YOU as a leader of a small mercenary group will lead your party to the glorious future and many treasures.

Remember the carefree days of gaming where games where created with utmost care with soul in them? These are the days we aim to bring back with the Reviclades. Dive into the wondrous world of mercenaries that change the course of history. You as a player create this game and contribute to the story that will come in the future.
