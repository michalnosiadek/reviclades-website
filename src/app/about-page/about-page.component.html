About us

We as a the Reviclades’ developers represent international cooperation between friends. Each of us hold a special place in our hearts for the video games. Willing to create something we can be proud of, we planned to develop the Reviclades as long as it takes. With only a bunch of manpower, we created a prototype of a game that is ready to play, but to create the game we dreamed of, we need players help to achieve it.

The Reviclades team consists of dedicated members experienced in programming, art and creative design. The idea for the game was born as a result of a player driven experience and a desire to undertake a challenge of creating something with pure passion. We are highly focused on every single player’s opinion to showcase a game that YOU want to play.

We started working on the Reviclades in 2021, when simple conversation gave a spark to a dream. With dedicated team of few people from around the world, we want to create true player driven experience.
